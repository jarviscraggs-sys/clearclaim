## SCRIPT 2: How-To Walkthrough (3–5 minutes)

**Tone:** Friendly, clear, professional. SaaS tutorial style.

---

### Section 1: Introduction (0–30s)

[SCENE] Screen recording of ClearClaim homepage. Cursor moves naturally. Clean desktop setup.

[VOICEOVER] Welcome to ClearClaim — the all-in-one construction management platform built for UK contractors. In this walkthrough, I'll show you everything: from getting set up, to approving invoices, calculating CIS, managing timesheets, and running your reports. Whether you're a main contractor with a team of subbies, or a growing construction business trying to ditch the spreadsheets — ClearClaim is built for you.

[TEXT ON SCREEN] ClearClaim — Full Platform Walkthrough

[MUSIC] Calm, professional background music. Low volume.

---

### Section 2: Contractor Signup & Setup (30s–1m 15s)

[SCENE] Screen recording: navigate to getclearclaim.co.uk. Click "Register Free". Select "I'm a Contractor".

[VOICEOVER] Let's start by registering as a contractor. Head to getclearclaim.co.uk and click "Register Free". Choose "I'm a Contractor" — this gives you the full contractor dashboard.

[SCENE] Fill in the registration form: company name, email, password.

[VOICEOVER] Fill in your company details — your company name, email address, and password. Once you verify your email, you'll land straight on your contractor dashboard.

[SCENE] Dashboard appears. Clean, dark UI. Sidebar visible.

[VOICEOVER] From here you can manage everything — invoices, subcontractors, employees, timesheets, and your CIS returns. Let's go through each one.

[TEXT ON SCREEN] Dashboard → Your command centre

[MUSIC] Continuous, calm.

---

### Section 3: Inviting Subcontractors (1m 15s–1m 45s)

[SCENE] Navigate to Subcontractors → Invite. A unique invite link is generated.

[VOICEOVER] Before your subbies can submit invoices, you need to invite them to your company. Head to Subcontractors, then click Invite. ClearClaim generates a unique invite link — just copy it and send it over WhatsApp, email, wherever.

[SCENE] Subcontractor registers via the link. Their name appears in the contractor's subcontractor list.

[VOICEOVER] When your subcontractor clicks the link, they register their own account — their details, their UTR number, their bank info. Once they're registered, they appear in your subcontractor list, ready to submit invoices.

[TEXT ON SCREEN] Invite Link → Sub registers → Linked instantly

[MUSIC] Continuous.

---

### Section 4: Submitting an Invoice (1m 45s–2m 45s)

[SCENE] Switch to subcontractor view. Navigate to Invoices → New Application for Payment.

[VOICEOVER] Now let's look at things from the subcontractor's side. Once they're logged in, they go to Invoices and create a new Application for Payment.

[SCENE] Sub fills in the invoice: project name, invoice number, description. Then adds job lines — labour, materials, plant.

[VOICEOVER] They fill in the basics — the project, invoice number, and description. Then they add their job lines. Maybe it's £5,000 for labour, £2,000 for materials, and £500 for plant hire. Each line is itemised.

[SCENE] Total updates automatically. Sub hits "Submit".

[VOICEOVER] The total updates automatically. When they're happy, they hit Submit — and instantly, the contractor gets notified.

[TEXT ON SCREEN] Application for Payment → Job lines → Submitted

[MUSIC] Continuous.

---

### Section 5: Reviewing & Approving (2m 45s–3m 30s)

[SCENE] Switch back to contractor view. Notification badge on Invoices. Click in — the invoice appears.

[VOICEOVER] Back on the contractor side, the invoice is waiting in your queue. You can see every line, the amounts, and the subcontractor's details.

[SCENE] Contractor hovers over a job line and clicks "Query". A comment box appears. Types: "Can you clarify the materials cost on this line?"

[VOICEOVER] If something doesn't look right — say a materials line looks high — you can query it. Type your question, and the subcontractor gets notified instantly.

[SCENE] Subcontractor view: they see the query, type a response, hit reply.

[VOICEOVER] The sub sees the query, types their response, and sends it back. Everything stays in one thread — no email chains, no WhatsApp confusion.

[SCENE] Back to contractor. Query resolved. Clicks "Approve".

[VOICEOVER] Once you're satisfied, click Approve — and ClearClaim takes it from there.

[TEXT ON SCREEN] Review → Query → Respond → Approve

[MUSIC] Continuous.

---

### Section 6: CIS & Payment Certificate (3m 30s–4m 00s)

[SCENE] After approval, a payment breakdown screen appears automatically. Shows: Gross, VAT, CIS deduction, Retention, Net Payment.

[VOICEOVER] The moment you approve, ClearClaim automatically calculates the CIS deduction — based on the subcontractor's verification status — along with VAT and any retention you've set. No manual maths. No spreadsheets.

[SCENE] A payment certificate PDF is generated. Shows "Certificate sent to subcontractor."

[VOICEOVER] A payment certificate is generated automatically and sent directly to the subcontractor as a PDF. HMRC-compliant, ready to file.

[TEXT ON SCREEN] CIS Auto-Calculated → Certificate Generated → Sent Instantly

[MUSIC] Continuous.

---

### Section 7: Timesheets & Holidays (4m 00s–4m 30s)

[SCENE] Navigate to Timesheets. An employee timesheet appears — grid with Mon to Sun, hours filled in.

[VOICEOVER] ClearClaim also handles your direct employees. In the Timesheets section, employees submit their hours for the week — each day filled in, totals calculated automatically. If anyone's worked overtime, it's flagged.

[SCENE] Contractor clicks Approve on the timesheet.

[VOICEOVER] You approve with one click.

[SCENE] Navigate to Holidays. A calendar view shows team availability. One day has a red capacity warning.

[VOICEOVER] Over in Holidays, you can see the full team calendar — who's off, when, and whether you've got enough cover. If too many people request the same day off, ClearClaim flags it.

[TEXT ON SCREEN] Timesheets → Approved | Holidays → Managed

[MUSIC] Continuous.

---

### Section 8: Dashboard & Reports (4m 30s–5m 00s)

[SCENE] Navigate to Dashboard / Reports. Stat cards visible: Total Invoiced, Pending Review, Retention Held, CIS Liability.

[VOICEOVER] Finally, the dashboard gives you a live view of your business. Total invoiced this month, what's pending review, how much retention you're holding — all at a glance.

[SCENE] Click into CIS Return report. Shows breakdown by subcontractor, deductions, ready to file.

[VOICEOVER] Run your CIS return report with one click — every deduction, every sub, ready to submit to HMRC.

[SCENE] Cash flow forecast chart — bars showing projected payments over coming weeks.

[VOICEOVER] And the cash flow forecast shows you what's coming in and when — so you're never caught short.

[TEXT ON SCREEN] Live Dashboard → CIS Return → Cash Flow Forecast

[MUSIC] Continuous.
